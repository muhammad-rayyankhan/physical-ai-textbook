# Introduction to Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to this interactive textbook on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics! This course will guide you through the fascinating world of intelligent physical systems.

## What You'll Learn

In this textbook, you'll explore:

- **Foundations**: The history and key concepts of Physical AI
- **Sensors & Perception**: How robots sense and understand their environment
- **Actuation & Control**: How robots move and interact with the physical world
- **Learning & Intelligence**: How AI enables robots to learn and adapt
- **Integration & Systems**: Putting it all together in real-world systems
- **Future & Ethics**: Emerging trends and ethical considerations

## Prerequisites

This course assumes:
- Basic understanding of computer science concepts
- Familiarity with programming (Python recommended)
- Interest in robotics and artificial intelligence

## Reading Time

This textbook is designed to be read in **under 45 minutes** total. Each chapter is concise and focused, allowing you to learn efficiently.

## How to Use This Textbook

- **Navigate**: Use the sidebar to jump between chapters
- **Learn**: Read through each chapter sequentially for best understanding
- **Practice**: Try the code examples provided in each chapter
- **Explore**: Follow the links and references for deeper learning

Let's begin your journey into Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics!
